The Quantum Design Hackathon brought together designers, developers, physicists, and curious minds to explore one question: how can we communicate the quantum world?

Teams had a single day to prototype something — a visualization, a musical experience, a game, a performance — that communicates a quantum computing qubit type (or concept) to a broad audience. The results ranged from drag-and-drop circuit simulators to a full audience-participation stage show.
